ACCESS MISSION AND VISION
OUR MISSION

The pursuit of a better quality of life for all Filipinos by the acquisition of the universal, basic education and skills, knowledge, attitudes and values necessary for himself, of his family, community and country.

OUR VISION

The holistic formation of Filipino citizens who will contribute to social transformation through human dignity, love for the poor, justice, peace and solidarity.
